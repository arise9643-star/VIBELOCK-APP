About Grinder

Grinder is a productivity platform designed to help teams and individuals maximize their focus and efficiency. Built on the proven Pomodoro technique, Grinder transforms ordinary work sessions into powerful focus sprints.

Our mission is to eliminate distractions, boost accountability, and create a culture of deep work. Whether you're coding, designing, studying, or collaborating, Grinder helps you stay in the zone and track your progress with precision.
